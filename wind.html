Wind Speed

The wind speeds have even distributions across latitudes, which implies that they are not affected by their position on Earth. There are a few significantly higher speeds in the northern hempisphere than the southern hemisphere, which could be attributed the the tilt of earth and its spheroid shape, or can even be affected by tidal and coriolis forces.
